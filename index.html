Drop your templates here

Brochures, flyers, one-pagers, signage, email layouts. Claude reads them, maps your listing fields into the placeholders, and edits them whenever you ask.

Recommended: .pptx (editable source). Also accepts .docx and .html. Drop a .pdf and Claude rebuilds it into an editable template.

Why PowerPoint, not PDF?

A PDF is finished and flattened — perfect for printing and sending, but hard to edit or restyle reliably. PowerPoint (.pptx) is structured: every text box, placeholder, and image is addressable, so Claude can change the layout and your listing data drops cleanly into the placeholders. Upload .pptx as your editable source; the finished material exports to PDF.
